## 0.2.0 — The writing surface

- **A real writing surface.** Rebuilt from the ground up: typewriter scrolling that keeps your active line centered, and sentence focus that dims everything but the line you're on.
- **Immersion mode.** Begin a session and Wall takes the whole screen — the net goes down, the menu bar disappears. (Toggle in Settings.)
- **Smart typography & markdown headings.** Curly quotes and em-dashes as you type; `#` headings styled live.
- **Archive, in the editor.** Browse and search everything you've written — now a tab in the main window (⌘L).
- **Choose where your writing lives.** Point Wall at a Dropbox or iCloud Drive folder in Settings.
